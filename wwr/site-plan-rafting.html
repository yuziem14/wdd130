Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to attract people to engage in rafting by providing essential information about this activity and sparking interest in trying or continuing to be involved in the sport. By offering rafting excursions and creating enjoyable experiences, we aim not only to increase the company's profits but also to make the business more sustainable and prosperous. In summary, our focus is to share information about the sport and build revenue.

Audience

The target audience comprises adults aged 18 to 50, regardless of financial situation or occupation, who are interested in trying rafting for the first time. Additionally, we target adults in the same age range who already engage in the sport and are seeking a place to enhance their skills. Both groups will access the website for essential information on rafting, available excursions, and affordable prices.
